3. Fighting for Family

Summary

In the very first episode, we're treated to one of the best moments in the series—when he sticks up for his family. While out shopping, a trio of degenerates make fun of his son (who struggles with cerebral palsy). Walt disappears out the back of the store, then re-enters from the front of the store to take the bullies by surprise. As he repeatedly stomps on one's leg, everyone in the shop is taken by surprise—including his family. The bully meekly cowers away and mutters: "Psycho." It's a characterization of Walt that ended up being... prophetic.
